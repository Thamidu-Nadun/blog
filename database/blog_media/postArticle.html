Blog Post Submission Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to our blog! We appreciate your desire to share your thoughts and expertise with our audience. To ensure a smooth and organized submission process, please follow these guidelines when sending your blog article via email to nadsoftlk@proton.me.

Submission Requirements:

1. Author Information

In your email, please provide the following author information:

  • Full Name: [John Doe]
  • Email Address: [johndoe@mail.com]
  • About You: [Share my knowledge with others]

You may also include a profile picture of yourself if you wish. This image should be attached to the email in a standard image format (JPEG, PNG, etc.) and should be appropriately sized for display on our blog.

2. Blog Article File

Attach your blog article as a separate file to the email. The acceptable file formats for your blog article are:

  • Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx)
  • Plain Text (.txt)

Please ensure that your article adheres to the following guidelines:

Article Format:

  • Use a clear and concise title at the beginning of your document.
  • Organize your article into sections or headings for better readability.
  • Use legible fonts and maintain a consistent font size throughout the document.
  • Include any relevant images within the document or provide separate image files if needed. Make sure to reference the image placements within the text.
  • Use hyperlinks when referencing external sources or websites.
  • Ensure proper grammar, spelling, and punctuation.

Article Content:

  • Content must be original and not published elsewhere.
  • Articles should be informative, engaging, and relevant to our blog's themes and target audience.
  • Avoid promotional or self-promotional content.
  • Respect copyright and give proper credit for any external content used.

Article Length:

Aim for a word count of 800-1500 words. Longer articles may be accepted if the content warrants it.

3. Submission Email Subject

In the subject line of your email, please use the following format: "Blog Submission - [Your Full Name]". This helps us quickly identify your submission.

Submission Process:

  1. Compose an email to nadsoftlk@proton.me with the required author information and attached blog article.
  2. Review your email to ensure that all necessary information is included.
  3. Send the email. Once we receive your submission, our editorial team will review it. If your article is accepted for publication, we will contact you to discuss the publication date and any potential edits.

Thank you for considering contributing to our blog. We look forward to receiving your valuable insights and sharing them with our audience. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us via the same email address.
